Your 7-Day Odyssey

Day 1: Arrival in Athens & Plaka Leisure

Arrive at Athens International Airport (ATH). Private transfer to your hotel near the historic Acropolis. Spend the evening exploring the charming cobblestone streets of Plaka.

Day 2: Ancient Athens Tour

Full-day guided tour of Athens' historical sites, including the majestic **Parthenon**, the **Ancient Agora**, and the grand **Temple of Olympian Zeus**.

Day 3: Flight to Santorini & Sunset in Oia

Morning domestic flight to Santorini (JTR). Check into your cliffside accommodation. Enjoy the afternoon at leisure before witnessing the iconic sunset over the Caldera in **Oia**.

Day 4: Volcano & Hot Springs Cruise

Full-day boat cruise exploring the volcanic islands. Swim in the **thermal hot springs** near Nea Kameni and relax on the beaches of Thirassia island.

Day 5: Ferry to Mykonos & Little Venice

Morning high-speed ferry to Mykonos. Transfer to your hotel. Later, explore the picturesque waterfront of **Little Venice** and the famous windmills.

Day 6: Archaeological Delos & Beach Leisure

Morning excursion to the nearby UNESCO World Heritage site of **Delos**, followed by a free afternoon for relaxation at one of Mykonos' famous beaches like Paradise or Psarou.

Day 7: Departure

Enjoy a final Greek breakfast before your private transfer returns you to Mykonos International Airport (JMK) for your international departure.
