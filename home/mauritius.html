Your 6-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Grand Bay Leisure

Arrive at Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam International Airport (SSR). Enjoy a private transfer to your 5-star resort near Grand Bay. Check-in and spend the evening relaxing by the ocean or exploring the resort's amenities.

Day 2: North Island Tour & Pamplemousses

Full-day tour of North Island, including the famous Pamplemousses Botanical Garden and the vibrant Port Louis waterfront. Enjoy shopping and a local lunch.

Day 3: Optional Catamaran to Île aux Cerfs

Choose an optional Catamaran cruise to the spectacular Île aux Cerfs, renowned for its white-sand beaches and turquoise lagoons. Enjoy water sports or simply sunbathe in paradise.

Day 4: South Island Wonders & Seven Coloured Earth

Explore the South with visits to the volcanic crater of Trou aux Cerfs, the sacred Ganga Talao (Grand Bassin), and the geological marvel of the Seven Coloured Earths at Chamarel.

Day 5: Deep Sea Relaxation / Free Day

A day reserved for personal leisure. Indulge in a spa session, try deep-sea fishing, or simply enjoy the resort's private beach before a farewell dinner.

Day 6: Departure

Enjoy a final breakfast before your private transfer returns you to the airport for your onward journey, filled with unforgettable memories.
