Your 7-Day Highland Journey

Day 1: Arrival in Edinburgh & Royal Mile

Arrive at Edinburgh Airport (EDI). Private transfer to your hotel. Spend the afternoon exploring the historic Royal Mile, the heart of the Old Town.

Day 2: Edinburgh Castle & Old Town Lore

Visit the magnificent **Edinburgh Castle** and take a guided tour of the Old Town. Optional evening: a spooky subterranean ghost tour.

Day 3: Journey to the Highlands (Fort William)

Scenic morning transfer through breathtaking landscapes to Fort William. Stop to view the famous **Glenfinnan Viaduct** along the way.

Day 4: Loch Ness & Monster Hunt

Full-day exploration of the Great Glen. Cruise on **Loch Ness** and visit the ruins of **Urquhart Castle** in search of the legendary Nessie.

Day 5: The Road to the Isle of Skye

Travel north, crossing the iconic Skye Bridge onto the **Isle of Skye**. Check into your hotel in **Portree** and explore the colourful harbor.

Day 6: Skye Majesty & Return South

Visit the dramatic **Old Man of Storr** and the cascading waterfall at **Kilt Rock**. Afternoon: Begin the scenic drive south towards Edinburgh.

Day 7: Departure

Enjoy a final full Scottish breakfast before your private transfer takes you to Edinburgh International Airport (EDI) for your international departure.
