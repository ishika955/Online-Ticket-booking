Your 7-Day Island Escape

Day 1: Arrival in Denpasar & Transfer to Ubud

Arrive at Denpasar Airport (DPS). Private transfer to your secluded villa in the spiritual heart of Bali, **Ubud**. Evening free to relax.

Day 2: Rice Terraces & Holy Water Temple

Morning visit to the stunning **Tegalalang Rice Terraces**. Afternoon: Experience a purification ritual at the sacred **Tirta Empul Temple**.

Day 3: Sunrise Walk & Monkey Forest

Wake up early for a rejuvenating walk along the **Campuhan Ridge**. Later, spend time observing the playful residents of the Ubud Monkey Forest.

Day 4: Coast Transfer & Tanah Lot Sunset

Morning transfer from Ubud to the chic beachfront area of **Seminyak**. In the evening, witness a spectacular sunset over the ocean temple of **Tanah Lot**.

Day 5: Beach Bliss & Cliffside Temple

Explore the beautiful southern beaches, including Padang Padang or Nusa Dua. Finish the day with a visit to the cliff-top **Uluwatu Temple**.

Day 6: Seminyak Leisure & Shopping

A full day at leisure. Enjoy your Seminyak resort pool, indulge in a Balinese spa treatment, or explore the area's upscale boutiques and cafes.

Day 7: Departure

Enjoy a final breakfast overlooking the pool before your private transfer takes you to Denpasar International Airport (DPS) for your international departure.
