Your 7-Day Coral Journey

Day 1: Arrival at Agatti & Lagoon View

Arrive at Agatti Aerodrome (AGX). Private transfer to your beach resort. Spend the afternoon relaxing by the stunning **Agatti Lagoon**.

Day 2: Transfer to Kavaratti & Capital Tour

Morning ferry transfer to **Kavaratti**, the capital island. Visit the Marine Aquarium and the local light house for panoramic views.

Day 3: Kavaratti Scuba & Snorkeling

Full day dedicated to water activities. Enjoy introductory **Scuba Diving** or snorkeling among the vibrant coral reefs and marine life.

Day 4: Transfer to Kalpeni & Thriving Reefs

Morning ferry to **Kalpeni Island**. Check into your hut and explore the vast, shallow lagoon, ideal for sunbathing and swimming.

Day 5: Pitti Island Kayaking & Water Sports

Excursion by boat to the uninhabited Pitti Island, known for its pristine beauty. Enjoy kayaking and other non-motorized **water sports**.

Day 6: Return to Agatti & Farewell Evening

Morning ferry back to Agatti. Enjoy a relaxed afternoon and a special **farewell dinner** on the beach under the stars.

Day 7: Departure

Enjoy a final view of the turquoise waters before your private transfer takes you to Agatti Aerodrome (AGX) for your flight back to Kochi.
